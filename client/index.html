LogicSim

Controls

  • Left click is same as a tap on mobile
  • Right click is same as a long press on mobile
  • Double left click is same as double tap on mobile
  • Left click buttons to add logic gates
  • Left click circuit/node and move around with cursor
  • Left click a node, then and drag cursor to another node to make a connection
  • Left click a node to toggle state
  • Left click sidebar to add an input/output
  • Right click on a gate or input/output node to rename it
  • Double left click on a gate or node to delete connection(s)
